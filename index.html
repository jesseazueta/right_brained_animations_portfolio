About

I am a professional 3D artist and web developer always looking towards the next project. Having a strong background in design, I decided to build on that skillset by learning how to program, making myself a hybrid of design and tech. My approach to any project is to "make the product work" and have fun while doing so. I have six plus years of experience and have worked with high end brand names such as Reebok, Northface, Timberland, and Vans to name a few. I am constantly learning and refining my skills to become a better artist AND developer. I am very excited to put my programming and design skills to use and create some innovative yet beautiful apps.

A creative at heart, I am in love with the creative process and what it entails. The idea of seeing an idea from concept to completion is challenging, yet exhilarating at the same time. It gives me the opportunity to not only be challenged, but to think differently and develop new processes. On the flipside, developing a technical skillset has become a vital necessityin my career; allowing me not just to think and put my ideas on paper, but to have the ability to bring some of them to life. Please feel free to browse through my portfolio to checkout some of my work. Thanks for stopping by and feel free to contact me whether it's a question, or if you just want to say hello!
